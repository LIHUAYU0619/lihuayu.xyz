June is a designer and artist currently based in New York City, working both independently and collaboratively with artists, designers, and curators. Her practice revolves around image-making and typography, with a primary focus on the production of books and visual identities.

Influenced by film, novels, and magazines, June is fascinated by seductive imagery and the unclassifiable, such as odradek. She graduated with an MFA from the Yale School of Art in 2024.
